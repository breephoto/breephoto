Welcome! My name is Bree,

I grew up the beautiful Denver, Colorado :) Life's unpredictable path has now landed me & my camera in Sacramento. While I'll always be a Colorado girl, California has become a huge part of me!

I have always loved taking pictures, my friends might tell you I take way too many, but I cant help it! I've been pursuing photography professionally for about 5 years, focusing mostly on people & 'lifestyle'. Thankfully my friends & family have been wonderful subjects for me to practice on. Capturing people in their true happiness, and creating memories they can appreciate forever is so important to me.

I stuidied photography & Photoshop editing at a studio in Mill Valley for about a year, other than that I am self taught and constantly learning from other Photographers who inspire me. Editing is my favorite part about the process! I love 're-living' the photoshoot, finding the most unique pictures and getting creative to bring out the best in the shot.

Life is naturally imperfect, fun & beautiful all in itself. I'm all about real life laughter and candid memories!
